The Impact of Mathematics Anxiety on Technology Career Choices

Several researchers have explored the level of anxiety perceived in Mathematics, but none have examined its influence on technology career selection. This study reveals misconceptions linked to mathematics anxiety in career decisions in tech. The results show that mathematics anxiety does not significantly influence willingness to pursue tech careers, particularly for individuals with higher education levels.

Analysing the Impact of Precipitation and Temperature on Cassava and Cocoa Crop Yields

This study investigates the influence of temperature and rainfall on cassava and cocoa yield in Ondo State from 1991 to 2020. The analysis used K-Means clustering and ANOVA to understand the relationship between climate variables and crop yields, revealing significant impacts of rainfall and temperature on crop production.
